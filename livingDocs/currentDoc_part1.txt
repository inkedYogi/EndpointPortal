N-Dimensional Spheres
The surface area and volume of n-spheres (unit spheres of dimension n) start to decrease in higher dimensions. 
More specifically, the volume of a given n-sphere begins to decrease after reaching the 5th-dimension, while the surface area of a given n-sphere begins to decrease after reaching the 7th-dimension.
